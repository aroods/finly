About Finly Portfolio Tracker

Finly is a lightweight, self-hosted portfolio tracker for individual investors and finance enthusiasts. It’s designed to run on your Raspberry Pi or home server, helping you keep full control over your data.

  • Track all your stocks, ETFs, bonds, crypto, and cash balances in one place.
  • Automatic updates of prices and currency rates.
  • Clear portfolio summaries, interactive charts, and P/L breakdowns.
  • Keep a full, editable transaction and cash history.
  • See upcoming events like dividends and earnings for your holdings.
  • All your data is private and stays on your device.

Built for easy use and extensibility—whether you’re tracking a few assets or your entire investment life!
